Solution Architecture

Evolvers recommends to first create an Enterprise Architecture compliance framework that is driven by improving the data integration, access, and security, common business processes, removal of duplicate system functionality, use of organization adopted standards, and reduced hardware and software license costs through shared Information Technology (IT) resources.

Each project executed by the organization or its vendors and partners would then be expected to follow and comply with the EA framework.

Our Solution Architecture consists of distinct layers, which allow any one layer to be changed at any time, for application to evolve in-sync with new technologies.

We are a big proponent of using Service Oriented Architecture (SOA). Some of the business drivers for SOA include the following:

  1. Reduced Integration Expense
  2. Increased Asset Reuse
  3. Increase Business Agility
  4. Reduced Business Risk

Geospatial Information Systems

In order to create a smart, long term city Geospatial Information Systems (GIS) strategy plan, we suggest taking a 360-degree view of city’s GIS-related resources. Evolvers will take people, processes, data assets, currently under-utilized potential in each area into account. Evolvers initial approach would be to understand the existing ‘eco systems’ of the city’s GIS data, source systems, access methods, usage levels. Once we uncover detailed current/projected requirements, we will offer technology specific plan. Evolvers strategies helps people to enable people to easily understand patterns and relationships.

Evolvers implementation with GIS project generally includes:

  • Roadmap
  • GIS Integration: Strategy for assimilating, integrating or interfacing different data sources
  • Security: Recommendations for how data can be secured
  • Governance: Recommendations for roles, responsibilities, structures, procedures for monitoring, defining and authorizing changes to existing data
