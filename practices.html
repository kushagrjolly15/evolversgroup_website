IT Contracting

Evolvers’ IT Contracting practice was established with the philosophy that, "The foundation of everything good is good people". Therefore, our investment in human resources is two-fold: first, our main investment is in our own recruiting staff, which in turn empowers them to hire the best talent for our clients; after all, it takes one to know one.
Our recruiting staff and contract resources are our best assets.
Our recruiters have technical background in diverse industries and undergo training in the core business area of new clients and their projected requirements.
Some of the core business areas where we are able to help in proving contract resources are:

    Information Technology
    Healthcare
    Manufacturing/Retail
    Sales, Marketing
    Financial and Banking
Our method of approaching challenges, problems and client engagement is based on our people's ability to match client needs with effective solutions.
Evolvers first applies a thorough understanding of the RFQ and RFP put before us. We scrutinize our clients' requirements and ensure that due diligence is given to fulfilling their needs. We ensure quality and technically advanced services to our clients by staying up-to-date on technology advancement.
We then dutifully screen our candidates with rigorous benchmarks and only present qualified expertise to our clients. Our committed staff accept the challenges put forth with every RFQ and RFP, and are at the ready to do what it takes to serve our clients.

Evolution Methodology

Evolvers was built on the foundation of strong software design, architecture and methodology

Project Management

Program/Project and Portfolio Management is the cornerstone of our Evolution™ methodology, and incorporates Project Management Institute® PMBOK guidelines, CMMI key process areas, Agile, industry best-practices and has plug-ins for client processes.

Risk Management

The risk management (RM) plan that we create at the onset of a project includes the Methodology, Roles and Responsibilities of the RM team, Budgeting for RM, Timing of when and how often the RM will be performed, Risk Categories, and Definitions of risk probability and impact.

Audit

The audit model of our Evolution Methodology™ helps assess post-implementation reviews, and audits of existing IT environments and teams. This approach is based on best practices developed from our vast experience, as well as some ISO and IEEE specifications.

Estimation/Forecasting

The estimation model helps us define a standard Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) and then we use it to build a repository from which to estimate and manage the cost and schedule of projects. We learn from every project and build the model to better estimate the next one.

Quality Assurance

We place strong emphasis on Quality Assurance (QA), Quality Control (QC) and Quality Improvement. The methodology utilizes tried and tested procedures, methods, models, guidelines and best practices.

Communication Plan

Our strength lies is in our understanding of how to use inclusion and communication into and throughout project life-cycle to keep the ownership’s buy-in and smooth adoption by all parties.
