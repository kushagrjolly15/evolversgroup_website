The Evolvers Group is a management and technology consulting firm that believes in the power of the idea. We deliver extraordinary value to clients by designing and executing innovative and profitable business and systems strategies through emergent technologies.

“To enable companies to evolve in-step with new technologies and to create market leadership by providing innovative technology and business enhancing solutions.”

Our team of Evolvers, comprising of business and management specialists, software designers, architects and methodologists, possesses experience in government (Federal, State and Local) and major industrial sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, telecommunications, aerospace, retail and finance.

At Evolvers, we believe that the solutions and services we provide should enable our clients to up on the value chain. Evolvers founders realized that the business processes, requirements and proper design played a very important role in developing solutions that could evolve with time with changing business needs and new technologies; were scalable, flexible, configurable, maintainable, and inter-operable.
